## Creating Your Own Journeys

The system responds to any word through phonetic analysis. Even made-up words create valid sonic experiences:

- **Vowels** (a, e, i, o, u) increase warmth and brightness
- **Hard consonants** (k, t, p, b, d, g) increase density and texture
- **Sibilants** (s, z, f, v, h) increase movement
- **Longer words** create more depth
- **Repeated letters** add emphasis

### Examples
- `shimmer` - soft sibilants, brightness
- `crackle` - hard consonants, texture
- `oolong` - round vowels, warmth
- `zephyr` - flowing sibilants, movement
